- Travel to restaurant
  - Uber
    - Open App
      - Select destination
        - Confirm ride
  - Public transportation
    - Bus
      - Check bus schedule
        - Walk to bus
          - Board bus
            - Pay fare
    - Train
     - Check train schedule
      - Walk to train
        - Pay fare
          - Board train
  - Drive
    - Unlock car
      - Get in vehicle
        - Start car
          - Drive to restaurant
            - Park on street
            - Valet car
              - Give valet car keys
                - Pay for valet parking
              - Get valet ticket
